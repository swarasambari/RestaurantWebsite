About Us

Welcome to our Mexican restaurant! We are a family-owned and operated establishment that takes great pride in serving delicious, authentic Mexican cuisine. Our mission is to provide a memorable dining experience for all of our customers. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create our mouth-watering dishes, and our menu features a variety of classic Mexican favorites as well as unique creations you won't find anywhere else. We believe that dining out should be more than just a meal - it should be an experience. That's why we have created a warm and inviting atmosphere that is perfect for date nights, family dinners, or even just a night out with friends. Our friendly staff is always happy to assist with any questions or recommendations, and we strive to make every customer feel at home. This is an initative by Swara, Atharva, Lavanya and Sahil- SALS a FIESTA [Salsa Fiesta]. Our objective is to introduce the authentic flavors of Mexico to India. With a passion for Mexican cuisine and culture, our team aims to provide a unique and enjoyable experience to their customers. Thank you for considering our restaurant for your next dining experience. We can't wait to welcome you and introduce you to the flavors of Mexico!
